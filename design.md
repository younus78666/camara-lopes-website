# Camara Lopes Clinic - Design Direction

## Color Palette
- **Navy Primary:** #1B3A5C (headings, nav, dark sections)
- **Navy Deep:** #0D1B2A (darkest backgrounds, footer)
- **Gold Accent:** #C8A456 (CTAs, highlights, badges, accent lines)
- **Gold Light:** #E8D5A3 (subtle gold tints)
- **Off-White:** #F7F5F0 (light section backgrounds)
- **Pure White:** #FFFFFF (cards, content areas)
- **Text Dark:** #1A1A1A (body text on light)
- **Text Muted:** #6B7280 (secondary text)
- **Text Light:** #E8E8E8 (body text on dark)

## Typography
- **Display/Headings:** Playfair Display (serif) — premium, medical, trustworthy
- **Body/UI:** DM Sans — clean, modern, readable
- **Hierarchy:** H1 4xl-6xl, H2 3xl-4xl, Section labels uppercase tracking-wider text-sm gold

## Layout Style (Nestora-inspired)
- Full-width hero with large image area
- Alternating light/dark sections
- Bento-grid style treatment cards
- Generous negative space
- Floating badges and credential strips
- Asymmetric doctor cards
- Large bold typography with editorial feel

## Motion
- Staggered fade-in on scroll (CSS-only with intersection observer)
- Smooth hover transitions on cards
- Subtle scale on hover for interactive elements

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Generic card grids
- Purple gradients
- Overused rounded corners
- Cookie-cutter layouts
